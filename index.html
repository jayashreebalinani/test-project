Welcome to 150+ Health!  We're happy you're here. What Information Do We Collect?
  • Your Information Stays on Your Phone: All your activity insights, like your activity levels or mood checks, stays right on your personal phone or tablet device. The only records we store long-term are summary statistics across entire groups to improve the app experience.  In other words, we retain information about you but only on an aggregated basis. You are not personally identified with the information. 

  • We See Group Information (in Summary): We do look at overall information from everyone using the app, like how many people are active or how everyone's mood is generally doing. We review individual data points in summary form. This helps us make the app better for everyone.  Again, this information is retained in the aggregate and not tied to any individual. 

  • We See Feedback You Provide: We review and retain any information you provide to us, including email and through the app, such as feedback about the app or troubleshooting.

  • Your Order Information Is Stored Securely: We store your name, email address, contact number, shipping address, and purchase details securely to support your order and ensure that only one purchase is allowed per account. This information is used solely for order processing, delivery, and customer support. 
Permissions and data access

To provide the best experience, we ask for access to some features on your device. You can always choose to allow or deny these permissions through your device’s settings.  

  • Camera Access: We request access to your camera to allow you to set up your profile picture and attach screenshots when providing feedback. 

  • Photo Library Access: We request access to your photo library to let you upload a profile picture and attach screenshots while submitting feedback. 

  • Apple Health Access (HealthKit): We access your Apple Health data, such as steps, heart rate and workouts, to enable activity tracking and offer a complete view of your health progress. 

  • Notification Access: Notification Access: We request permission to send you timely reminders and updates related to your activities, health progress, and app features. 

  • Location Access (if enabled): We use your location to help you find nearby pickleball courts and other relevant services. This permission is optional and can be turned off at any time. 
Payments and Financial Information

  • We do not share personal data with Stripe; it collects only what's needed to complete the transaction.

But again, it's just group information – not yours personally.
